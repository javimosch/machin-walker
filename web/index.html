machin-walker

A tinybrain neural network (13 sensors → 16 → 4, one ~4.7 KB JSON artifact) drives six torque-limited joints — and its own gait clock — of a 1.75 m / 70 kg biped through a rigorous pure-machin physics simulation, compiled to WebAssembly. Internal joint torques only — no balance assist, no velocity caps, momentum-conserving motors, Coulomb friction. It learned two certified gaits: a strict walk (0.54 m/s, 44% double-support, under 8% airborne) and a run (~1.5 m/s, flight-phase) — each with zero falls on noise seeds never seen in training.
