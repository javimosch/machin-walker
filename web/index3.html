machin-walker

A tinybrain neural network (13 sensors → 16 → 4, one ~4.7 KB JSON artifact) drives six torque-limited joints — and its own gait clock — of a 1.75 m / 70 kg biped through a rigorous pure-machin physics simulation, compiled to WebAssembly. Internal joint torques only — no balance assist, no velocity caps, momentum-conserving motors, Coulomb friction. This is the FULL-3D biped: lateral balance and heading are simulated, not staged — the depth you see is physics. Zero falls on noise seeds never seen in training; a strict low-flight walk remains the open milestone. The physics below runs in your browser; JS only draws.
